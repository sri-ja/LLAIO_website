International Linguistics Olympiad

The International Linguistics Olympiad (IOL) is an annual international competition that brings together secondary school students and experts from various fields of linguistics. The IOL encourages creativity and imagination and helps participants develop skills in language analysis and problem-solving. By promoting awareness and understanding of diverse cultures and histories, the competition plays a vital role in nurturing future linguistics experts and contributing to the development of this academic field.

— Learn more about IOL

Roadmap to IOL

The Panini Linguistics Olympiad (PLO) is a national competition that serves as India's qualifier for the International Linguistics Olympiad (IOL). The process begins with an initial round, where participants solve linguistic puzzles. Top performers then advance to an Invitational Round, where they receive further training. Those excelling at this stage are selected to represent India at the IOL. The competition offers a platform for school students to showcase their linguistic abilities and a chance to advance to the international stage.

International AI Olympiad

The International Olympiad in Artificial Intelligence (IOAI) (ioai-official.org) is a global competition that aims to identify and nurture young talent in the field of AI. It brings together school students from across the world to compete in solving complex AI challenges, fostering creativity and innovation.

— Learn more about IAIO

Roadmap to IAIO

The India part of the IOAI serves as the qualifying competition for IOAI. This national-level event tests participants' AI/machine-learning skills and also the problem solving skills in general. The Indian competition aims at selecting a team from India to take part in the next edition of IOAI to be held in Beijing, China, in the summer of 2025. The competition offers a platform for school students from India to showcase their technical abilities in AI and a chance to advance to the international stage.
